About IASA

Eastern Michigan University's Information Assurance Student Association (IASA) is pleased to hold veteran and underclassmen students!- Come hangout at the IASA meetings every Friday at 5:30pm! (Unless informed otherwise via email or slack)

The objective of this student association is to inspire students to practice their abilities within a virtual enviroment, gain connections with fellow students, and eat pizza! Meetings will consist of lectures, hands on practice, and student interactions.

The meetings are held at the Roosevelt Hall, in room 6 698 W Forest Ave #626, Ypsilanti, MI 48197.
