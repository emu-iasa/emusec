About IASA

Eastern Michigan University's Information Assurance Student Association (IASA) is pleased to hold veteran and underclassmen students!- IASA meetings every Friday at 5:30pm! (Unless informed otherwise via email or slack)

The objective of this student association is to inspire students to practice their abilities within a virtual enviroment and gain connections with fellow students. Meetings will consist of lectures, hands on practice, and student interactions.

The meetings are held at Roosevelt Hall, in room 6 698 W Forest Ave #626, Ypsilanti, MI 48197.
