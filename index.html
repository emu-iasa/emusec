About IASA

Eastern Michigan University's Information Assurance Student Association (IASA) is pleased to hold veteran and underclassmen students!- Come hangout at the IASA meetings every Friday at 5:30pm! (Unless informed otherwise via email or slack)

The objective of this student association is to inspire students to practice their abilities within a virtual enviroment, gain connections with fellow students, and eat pizza! Meetings will consist of lectures, hands on practice, and student interactions.

The meetings are held at the Roosevelt Hall, in room 6 698 W Forest Ave #626, Ypsilanti, MI 48197.

Werbung: Pure Black GmbH

Weekly Meetings

Week 1 (Week 1 slides)

Septemeber 13, 2019

Hello everyone,

On behalf of the Information Assurance Student Association, we welcome you all back from the summer! We're really hoping to make this year a good one, and we'd love for you to join us in it!

We're the IASA, a student led organization focused around Cyber Security. We work on projects, lab, and technology you won't get in the classroom. We then take what we know and travel to participate in several competitions both locally and around the U.S. If you're interested in bettering your knowledge in all things Cyber Security, join us this Friday at 5:30 in Roosevelt Room 6 for pizza and more information. We'll be talking about who we are as an organization, what we do during meetings, and the competitions we go to.

Note: You don't have to be an expert in cyber security to participate! Our members range from very experienced people working the field to people who just entered the program. Nobody knows everything, especially in this field, and we're here to help you get there.

If you would like more information, feel free to email me at cmeyer25@emich.edu or put questions in our Slack channel at emusec.slack.com. To install Slack, either grab the Slack app on the Google Play Store or App Store, or go to https://slack.com/downloads/

See you Friday,

~The IASA Team
